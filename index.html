Interfaz visual interactiva de Residuos Sólidos Urbanos en CDMX




The Interactive visual interface presents the flow diagram system from the Urban Solid Waste in Mexico City (CDMX), according with Solid Waste's inventory published in 2016 by Secretaría del Medio Ambiente (SEDEMA). The job is been made possible in collaboration with Patricia Galán Lara.

Interfaz visual interactiva de Residuos Sólidos Urbanos en CDMX

How many Urban Solid Waste flows through each process station according with the quantities published by SEDEMA?


As a reference, the stroke Weight of each pipeline represents the quantity of solid waste, according with the information available. The data was scaled divide between ten (e.g. 1,038.23 ton/day, to obtain 103.82 and take only the integer value. For now).
